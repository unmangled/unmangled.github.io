Beware the Mangle Mites!

Throughout the history of the Internets, websites have fallen victim to mangle mites. If left untreated, they can pollute your website traffic to the point of affecting your revenues. They are are an elusive species!

What are mangle-mites?

They have many names: Internet robots, WWW robots, or simply bots.
